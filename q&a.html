Frequently Asked Questions

What is a vintage rally club?

A vintage rally club is a company that hosts car rally events annually.
When do car rally events of vintage rally club usually take place?

Car rally events usually take place at the end of September.
How much does it cost to enter the rally event?

It costs about $150 per person for the entrance fee.
What do I need to participate in the rally event?

You will need a car insurance and a driver's license.
Is there an age limit to compete in the rally event?

Yes, you should be over 18 to be able to compete in the event.
